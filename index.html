About

From operations floor to production code

I'm a full-stack and mobile developer who ships AI products end-to-end — from idea to architecture to production deployment. I currently work as Co-Founder & CTO of HuntForTomorrow, a B2C AI job-search SaaS platform, where I lead engineering across the web app, AI integrations, and infrastructure.

Before going full-time into software, I spent 7+ years in operations and analytics at Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL), a Fortune 500 company — experience that shaped how I think about reliability, process, and building systems that hold up under real-world pressure. Today I build across web, mobile, AI, and fintech, with a strong bias toward shipping things that work.

  • Operations background — built for reliability, not just demos
